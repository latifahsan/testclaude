Hi, I'm Latif 👋

I'm a vocational high school student majoring in Teknik Jaringan Komputer dan Telekomunikasi (TJKT), with a genuine passion for IT support, computer troubleshooting, and networking.

My journey started with a simple curiosity — why do computers break, and how do you fix them? That curiosity evolved into a skill set, an internship, and now a portfolio of projects I'm truly proud of.

I believe in learning by doing. Every service call, every broken device, and every network I've configured has taught me something new. I'm driven by the motto: "Belajar, Berkarya, dan Bertumbuh Setiap Hari."
